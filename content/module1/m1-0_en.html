Course Structure

This course is divided into 8 units:

Unit 1 provides an overview of the course.

Unit 2 provides an overview of the ICS System.

Unit 3 identifies and describes the basic features of ICS.

Unit 4 explains the functions of the Incident Commander & Command Staff.

Unit 5 describes the functions of the General Staff.

Unit 6 identifies and explains the standardized ICS Facilities.

Unit 7 explains common responsibilities for all ICS personnel.

Finally, Unit 8 provides a brief course summary and the final exam.
