Welcome to the Introduction to Incident Command System (ICS) I-100 course

Objectives:

  • To demonstrate basic knowledge of the Incident Command System

  • To learn the principles of ICS and the basic ICS structure and terminology

  • To learn overall incident management skills rather than tactical expertise

Introduction to ICS is an orientation for personnel assisting at an incident or event, persons working in support roles, and off-incident personnel who require a minimum of ICS understanding. There is no prerequisite for this course.

Introduction to ICS introduces the Incident Command System, and provides the foundation for higher level ICS training. This course describes the history, features and principles, and organizational structure of the Incident Command System.

This course can be completed in two to four hours of self-paced study time.

As you go through the course, we invite you to visit the ICS Canada website if you are interested in viewing the various ICS forms or other ICS materials.
