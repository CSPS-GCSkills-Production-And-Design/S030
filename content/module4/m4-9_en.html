Safety Officer


The Safety Officer:

  • Monitors safety conditions

  • Assesses hazardous and unsafe situations

  • Develops measures to assure safety of all assigned personnel

  • Advises the Incident Commander on issues regarding incident safety

  • Has emergency authority to stop unsafe acts if personnel are in imminent danger

  • Works with Operations Section to ensure the safety of tactical and support personnel

When assigned, only one Safety Officer is named to an incident. The Safety Officer may have assistants as necessary, and the assistants may represent other agencies or jurisdictions. A safety officer is not required on every incident.
