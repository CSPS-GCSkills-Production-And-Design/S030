Deputy Incident Commander

The Incident Commander may appoint one or more Deputies, if necessary, from the same agency or from other agencies or jurisdictions.​​​​​​​

  • Perform specific tasks as requested by IC

  • Perform the IC function in a relief capacity

  • May represent an assisting agency that shares jurisdiction

Note: If a deputy is assigned, he or she must be fully qualified to assume the Incident Commander’s position.​​​​​​​
