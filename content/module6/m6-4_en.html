Incident Base

This is the map symbol for the Base.

Incident Base:

  • Primary logistics functions

  • One Base per incident

  • Designated by incident name

  • Established and managed by Logistics Section

  • any tactical resources assigned to the incident base are always “out of service”

  • May co-locate with ICP

Many structural fire departments have a vehicle dedicated to a supply of breathing air and lighting as well as the rehabilitation of firefighters. This could also be designated as a Base.
