Incident Facilities

 

Incident facilities are established by the Incident Commander, depending on the requirements and complexity of the incident or event.

  • First priority is the needs of the incident

  • Not all incidents will use all facilities and specific applications may make use of other facilities.

  • Activated only when needed

    • Needs of the incident

    • Length of time

    • Cost

    • Environmental considerations

  • Specialized facilities

6 Basic Facilities

  • Incident Command Post (ICP)

  • Staging Area(s)

  • Base

  • Camp(s)

  • Helibase(s)

  • Helispot(s)
