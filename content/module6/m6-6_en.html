A Helibase is the main location within the general incident area from which helicopter-centered air operations are conducted. It is generally used on a more long term basis, and includes such services as fueling and maintenance.

Helibase:

  • Park, fuel, service and load helicopters

  • Located at airport or off-incident location

  • Designated by name of incident for example Sofa Mountain Helibase

  • May have more than one, for example a second Helibase would be called Sofa Mountain Helibase #2

  • Normally not moved

  • Helibase Manager
