Helispot

This is the map symbol for Helispot.

Helispot:

  • Temporary location in the incident area where helicopters can safely take off and land. As the visual shows, a Helispot would be the location in a field or on a highway that a medevac helicopter would land in order to receive patients.

  • Load or off-load personnel, equipment and supplies

  • May be more than one, which would be numbered sequentially as they are identified.

  • Helispot Manager

  • Reports to the Helibase Manager, if a Helibase is established. If an incident has not established air operations organization, but does have one or more Helispots designated, the Helispot Managers report to the Operations Section Chief.
