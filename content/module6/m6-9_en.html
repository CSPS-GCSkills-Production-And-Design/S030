Summary


To summarize: Review each of the symbols for a summary of that facility.

S

A Staging Area is where available incident personnel and/or equipment await tactical assignment.

B

The Base is the location where primary logistics and administrative functions are coordinated and administered, and where out-of-service resources are kept.

C

Camps are where food, water, rest and sanitary services are provided to incident personnel.

ICP

The Incident Command Post (ICP) is the location at which primary command functions are performed and where the Incident Commander can be found. Usually located with the incident Base.

H

A Helibase is where parking, fueling, maintenance and loading of helicopters occurs.

H-1

A Helispot is a temporary location at an incident where helicopters can safely land and take off.
