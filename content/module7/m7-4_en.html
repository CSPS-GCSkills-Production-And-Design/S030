Deployment Briefing

 

The Deployment Briefing that you receive prior to departure should familiarize you with any information related to the incident including but not limited to the following points.

  • Descriptive location and response area

  • Incident check-in location

  • Specific assignment

  • Reporting time

  • Communications instructions

  • Special support requirements, regarding issues such as facilities, equipment transportation and offloading

  • Travel arrangements
