Record Keeping

 

Another responsibility common to all ICS personnel is Record Keeping. Please click on the questions below for more information.

Questions :

What are the challenges for maintaining records at an incident?

Answer

  • Incident recordkeeping is important for the transfer of command.

  • When a new Incident Commander arrives on the scene, he or she will need to know as much as possible about what has occurred in the previous operational period.

  • Risk management - legal records.

Why is incident recordkeeping important?

Answer
  • Maintaining records at an incident can be challenging because personnel are often under stress in a fast moving and dynamic situation.
