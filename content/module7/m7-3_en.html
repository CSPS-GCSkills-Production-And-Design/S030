Dispatch and Deployment

 

It is important for all personnel working in ICS to remember considerations regarding dispatch, deployment and travel guidelines for extended stay or out-of-jurisdiction incidents.

Dispatch and Deployment:

  • Only deploy when requested or dispatched by appropriate authority

  • Avoid self-dispatching or freelancing

  • Make sure you receive a complete deployment briefing

 

Extended Stay or Out-of-Jurisdiction Travel Guidelines:

  • Assemble / update a travel kit containing any special technical information, such as maps, manuals, contact lists, and other reference materials

  • Prepare personal items required for the estimated length of stay

  • Ensure family members know destination and how to contact you in the event of a personal emergency

  • Familiarize yourself with any information related to the incident prior to departure by receiving a complete deployment briefing
