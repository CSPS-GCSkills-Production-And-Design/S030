Common Responsibilities

 

There are a number of common responsibilities that will apply to all ICS personnel on all incidents.

Common Responsibilities:

  • Bring specialized supplies

  • Follow check-in procedure

  • Use plain language on radio

  • Obtain briefing from immediate supervisor

  • Acquire work materials; locate and set up work area

  • Organize and brief subordinates

  • Brief your relief

  • Complete forms / reports

  • Demobilize according to plan
