Check-in Purpose

 

Another important process for all ICS personnel is check-in. It is critical to always check in at an incident. Check-in officially logs you in, and provides important basic information which will be used for status keeping, as well as for release and demobilization.

Check-in information is used in several ways at the incident, and supports the following activities:

  • Ensures personnel accountability

  • Tracks resources

  • Prepares personnel for assignments and reassignments

  • Locates personnel in case of emergency

  • Establishes personnel time records and payroll documentation

  • Supports release planning and demobilization
