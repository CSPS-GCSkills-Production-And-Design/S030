Instructions for Final Exam

 

In order to successfully complete this training, and receive your ICS, I-100 certificate, you must complete and submit the following final exam.

The exam consists of 25 multiple choice questions (Multiple Choice and True or False).

There is no time limit to complete this exam.

You are welcome to access any of the course content before and/or during the exam.

Be sure to read each question carefully.

A minimum of 80% is required in order to receive the ICS, I-100 certificate.

Good luck!
