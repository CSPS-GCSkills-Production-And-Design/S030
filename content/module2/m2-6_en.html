Purposes of ICS


Purposes of ICS:

  • Safety of responders and others by emphasizing span of control, Unity of Command, and Chain of Command. If the incident warrants, there is also the opportunity to provide for a dedicated Safety Officer position, which contributes to the overall safety of the incident
  • Achievement of incident objectives by ensuring that every incident gets an Incident Action Plan, either verbal or written, that has measurable actions
  • Efficient use of resources by utilizing Staging Areas, Incident Action Plans, span of control and Unity of Command

ICS is a proven management system and is the result of decades of lessons learned. It has been tested in more than 40 years of application, by all levels of Government and in the private sector. It represents organizational “best practices” and is becoming the standard command and control structure for emergency management across Canada and internationally.
