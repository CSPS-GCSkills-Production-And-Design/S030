What is an Incident?

 

An incident is an occurrence, natural or human-caused, that requires a response to protect life, property or the environment.

The ICS system is used to manage emergencies or incidents as well as non-emergency events such as parades or concerts. It can be used equally well for both small and large situations.

Applications for the use of ICS

Applications for the use of ICS

Possible applications for the use of ICS include, but are not limited to:

  • Firefighting, both wildland and structural

  • Natural disasters such as; tornadoes, floods, ice storms, or earthquakes

  • Human and animal disease outbreaks

  • Search and rescue missions

  • Hazardous materials incidents

  • Train derailments, marine vessel groundings and aircraft accidents

  • Private sector emergency management programs

  • Oil spill response and recovery incidents

  • Criminal acts and crime scene investigations

  • Chemical, biological, radiological, nuclear and explosive incidents

  • Planned events such as parades, demonstrations, sporting events, or concerts
