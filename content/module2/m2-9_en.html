Benefits of ICS

 

ICS is designed to be interdisciplinary and organizationally flexible.

  • Safety of responders by allowing for a Safety Officer and maintaining Span of Control and Unity of Command as well as putting Life safety forward as the number one priority in incident management

  • Effective use of resources by utilizing Span of Control concepts, Staging Areas, Incident Objectives and an Incident Action Plan

  • Avoids duplication by utilizing a well defined action planning process, melding all responding agencies into one management structure and Unity of Command

  • Meets all incident needs by providing a structure for logistical and administrative support
