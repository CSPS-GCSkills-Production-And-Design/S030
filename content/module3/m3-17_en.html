Summary


In Summary, ICS:

  • Utilizes management features , including the use of common terminology and a modular organizational structure

  • Utilizes the principles of:

    • Chain of Command

    • Unity of Command

    • Unified Command

    • Transfer of Command

  • Emphasizes effective planning through the use of management by objectives and Incident Action Plans

  • Supports responders by providing data they need through effective information and intelligence management

  • Helps ensure that resources are ready through accountability and dispatch / deployment processes

  • Ensures full utilization of incident resources by maintaining a manageable span of control, establishing pre-designated incident facilities, implementing resource management practices, and ensuring integrated communications
