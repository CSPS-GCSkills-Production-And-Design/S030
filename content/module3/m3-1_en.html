Unit Objectives


At the end of this unit, you will be able to:

List the 14 basic principles of ICS based on 6 features:

  • Standardization

  • Command

  • Planning / Organization Structure

  • Facilities and Resources

  • Communications / Information Management

  • Professionalism

This is a very important Unit of the Canadian ICS training curriculum. If you have a good grasp of ICS principles and features, you will be able to make effective use of the system whenever and wherever you apply it.
