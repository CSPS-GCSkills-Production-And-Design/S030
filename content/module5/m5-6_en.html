ICS Section Chiefs and Deputies

Section Chiefs

  • The person in charge of each Section is designated as a Chief.

  • Section Chiefs have the ability to expand their Section to meet the needs of the situation.

Deputies

  • Deputies may assume responsibility for a specific portion of the primary position, work as relief, or be assigned to other tasks.

  • Deputies should always be as proficient as the person for whom he or she works. Must always be fully qualified or certified in the primary position.

Each of the Section Chiefs may have a Deputy, or more than one if necessary.

In large incidents, especially where multiple disciplines or jurisdictions are involved, the use of Deputies from other organizations can greatly increase interagency cooperation.
