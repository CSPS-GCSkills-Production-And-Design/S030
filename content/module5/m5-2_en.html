General Staff

Expansion of an incident may require the performance of the other management functions. The people who perform the other four management functions are designated as the General Staff.

The general staff is made up of four Sections, each of which is lead by a Section Chief. These include the Operations Section Chief, the Planning Section Chief, the Logistics Section Chief and the Finance/Administration Section Chief.

Each member of the General Staff reports directly to the Incident Commander.
